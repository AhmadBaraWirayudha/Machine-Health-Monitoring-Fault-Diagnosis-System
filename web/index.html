Diagnostic Features Extracted

Time-domain and frequency-domain indicators for machine condition

📈

RMS (Root Mean Square)

Overall vibration energy level. Rises with increasing fault severity and bearing wear.

⚡

Kurtosis

Statistical impulsiveness of the signal. Values >4 indicate bearing fault onset.

🏔

Crest Factor

Peak / RMS ratio. Detects early-stage shock events from bearing defects.

🌊

FFT Peak Frequency

Dominant frequency in the spectrum. Maps to shaft, bearing, or gear fault frequencies.

🎯

Band Energy

Spectral energy split into low / mid / high bands for targeted fault isolation.

🔢

Spectral Entropy

Frequency complexity. Low entropy = narrow-band fault tone. High = broad noise.
