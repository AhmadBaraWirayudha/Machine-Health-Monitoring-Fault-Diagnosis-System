2.2 Feature Extraction

Diagnostic features were extracted from sensor readings using both time-domain and frequency-domain methods:

  • RMS — Root Mean Square: overall vibration energy level
  • Kurtosis — impulsive content; elevated values indicate bearing defects
  • Crest Factor — Peak / RMS; detects early-stage shock events
  • FFT Peak Frequency — dominant fault frequency in the spectrum
  • Band Energies — spectral energy in low / mid / high frequency bands
  • Spectral Entropy — signal complexity indicator

2.3 Health Scoring Model

{{ health_scoring_description }}

The Asset Health Score (AHS) is a weighted combination of normalised feature scores:

  • RMS score: 25%
  • Kurtosis score: 25%
  • Crest Factor score: 20%
  • Anomaly detector score (Isolation Forest): 30%
